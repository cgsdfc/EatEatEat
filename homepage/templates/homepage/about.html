Fast Food should be Fast

The time you have to wait for the arrival of the food should be minimized. Waiting is a waste of your precious time to get your food. Now that you are that busy to have fast food, we dare not waste you a single minute. For that, we optimize every stage of the process to bring the food to you as fast as possible.

As many varieties as possible

The variety of fast food should be as vast as possible. The makers of this site were deeply addictive to fast food when they were working for a Lab as their life was too busy for a well schedualed meal at the school canteen. What's worse, at that time the available kinds of dishes and restaurants were very limited, so their receipts had to repeat very often, which hurt their appetite. We made this site to prevent tragedy like this from happening on you once and for good.
